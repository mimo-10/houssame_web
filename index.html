  • Freight Rate Monitoring Dashboard – Holcim (Logistics & Marketing)

    Professional

    Project Type: Professional Project



    Description:
    •Developed a complete analytical tool to support Holcim’s marketing team in understanding and tracking transportation cost variations by city.


    Context :
    •The marketing department needed a simple, unified visualization of Freight Rate trends (DZD/tonne), which previously existed through scattered reports managed only by logistics.

    Objective :
    •Create a clear, interactive dashboard that identifies abnormal variations by city and highlights possible causes (e.g., transporters).

    Approach :
    •Applied an automatic filter to keep only cities with more than 1000 tonnes delivered, ensuring reliable insights.
    •Implemented a linear regression analysis to calculate the trend (slope) of Freight Rate over time for every city.
    •Created a custom DAX measure (least-squares method) to compute the variation slope.
    •Identified significant increases/decreases using statistical thresholds (IQR-based filtering, slope outside [-0.8, +0.8]).

    Result :
    •Delivered an interactive dashboard adopted directly by marketing.
    •Enabled rapid detection of abnormal Freight Rate variations.
    •Improved transparency between logistics and marketing.
    •Empowered marketing to interpret cost fluctuations independently, with city-by-city diagnostic capability.

    Technologies: Power BI, DAX, Analytics
